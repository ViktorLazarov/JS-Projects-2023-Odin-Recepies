Crunchy Ramen Broccoli Slaw

Description

A delicious chinese ramen with fresh ingredients and herbs!

Easy to make, without any special techniques!

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup butter
  • 2 (3 ounce) packages Oriental-flavor ramen noodle soup, seasoning packet reserved
  • ½ cup slivered almonds
  • ¼ cup sesame seeds
  • 1 (16 ounce) package broccoli coleslaw mix
  • ½ cup chopped green onion
